Corona Virus Symptoms

What are the symptoms?

People may be sick with the virus from 1 to 14 days before developing symptoms. The most common symptoms of coronavirus disease (COVID-19) are fever, tiredness and dry cough. Most people (about 80%) recover from the disease without needing special treatment.More rarely, the disease can be serious and even fatal. Older people, and people with other medical conditions (such as asthma, diabetes, or heart disease), may be mor vulnerable to becoming severely ill.

Most common symptoms:
Dry cough Tiredness Fever
Less common symptoms:
Aches and Pains Sore Throat Diarrhoea Conjunctivitis Headache Loss of taste or smell A rash on skin or discolouration of fingers or toes
Serious symptoms:
Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath Chest pain or pressure Loss of speech or movement

How can I protect myself from COVID-19?

Coronavirus disease spreads primarily through contact with an infected person when they cough or sneeze. It also spreads when a person touches a surface or object that has the virus on it, the touches their eyes, nose or mouth.

What should I do?
Wear a face mask
Don't touch your face
Avoid hand contact
Keep safe distance
Stay at home if you can
